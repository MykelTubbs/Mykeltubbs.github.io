Mykel Tubbs

Early Life

I was born November 19, 1999 in Fayettville North Carolina. Born into a military family , I was forced to move around as a child. My mother and I moved to Rhode Island for a few years before finally moving to my permanant home of Atlanta, Georgia. My immediate family is small, but I do have a little sister named Willow and a dog named Taz.

I started playing football at the age of 8 and haven't looked back since. My family immediately knew I possessed a gift to play the sport and ensured I went to various football camps. This process would continue until I reached highschool and continued to excel.

High School

I had the opportunity to attend one of the prestigious highschool's in Georgia and excelled. In my freshman year I quickly made a presence on and off of the football field. I started on varsity and made my name known around the state getting interest from many major D1 colleges. After a great freshman year, all the major schools had me on their radar but unfortunately I tore my ACL and missed alot of time.

Triumph

While it was devasting to sustain an injury like that, I didn't allow it to stop me. Unfortunately many of the major D1 schools backed off, but i stil was able to get back healthy and earn 15 D1 scholarships. I eventually signed with the Air Force Academy and played for them for a year before transferring to the University Of Miami.
